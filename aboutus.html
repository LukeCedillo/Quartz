Why are we doing this?


Why ARE we doing this? We are just a small team of no pay coders and we aren't getting anywhere anyways.. so why?

Well it's because we are coders AND animators who just want a reliable software that won't cost the GDP of a small country to use!

This is a passion project and all of us had a lot of fun making this software and we hope that we can continue to improve Quartz and protect our free, open-source, animation software!

Created by animators for animators!!! (I know it's cliche but still!)
